How To Play

Players try to guess the word common to the "cue" words appearing on the screen. It starts with just 2 cue words (20 seconds), a tough challenge. If unsolved, a 3rd cue appears (15 more seconds), then a 4th (10 seconds) and finally a 5th (5 seconds). The quicker the solution, the more points earned.

Answers appear after a correct solution. There are 10 rounds to a game, after which players' points are shown for that game. Your cumulative score for the session is in the box at the top. You can play as many games as you want. Have fun!
