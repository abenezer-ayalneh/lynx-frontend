How To Play Lynx

The challenge is to guess the word common to all of the words you see on the screen. You’ll see 3 “cue” words or phrases that all share that same unknown word. You will compete to guess the word first before the time ends or other player guesses it.
Players have 15 seconds to enter their guesses. If no solution is found, a 4th cue drops (with 10 more additional seconds). Still stuck? A 5th cue appears (with additional 5 seconds). The answers appear after the correct solution has been entered by one of the players or when time finally expires after the 5th cue's additional seconds. A winner’s points depend on speed of their solution. There are 5 rounds to a game, followed by a tally of all players’ points. You can play as many games as you’d like.
